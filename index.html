ES Modules Support

There seem to be some competing thoughts and proposals amongst the Node.js Community as they move towards adopting a static synchronous import paradigm.
In the absence of a true definitive guide,

  • this project uses the '*.mjs' filename convention called out in Native ES Modules in NodeJS.
  • its package.json has a "module" property,
    though its use may be incorrect since the In Defense of .js proposal may or may not be current.
  • import capabilities are provided by the esm package,
    vs. the "native command line option" mentioned in Dr. Rauschmayer's article on Using ES modules natively in Node.js.
